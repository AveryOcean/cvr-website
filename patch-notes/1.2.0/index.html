Compensation VR version 1.2.0

Major Updates

We've added a new map!

You can find the Island map in the Home menu of your Watch! We hope this will become a cozy social spot, or somewhere nice to chill out & relax.

We've added Messaging!

You can now message other players in a shared social hub, based off the likes of Discord and Guilded! This will soon be accessable from out of game.
At the moment, there is only an official server you can access. We plan to add custom servers, DMs, group chats, and more in the future.

The Capture Camera

We've added a new tool for those of you who like to capture the world around you. You can now use the Capture Camera to take snapshots of the world around you.
The camera supports Image capture (with video soon to come), and is PLATFORM AGNOSTIC!

Images are currently saved to our servers, and soon you will be able to view them online.
Additionally, you can adjust the FOV, width, and height of your images using the settings menu.

New Keyboard Layout

To go along with the Social menu, we've added a full en-US keyboard layout to both the login keyboard and the watch keyboard!
This should be useful for messaging, as well as the long awaited ability to put return characters in your bio!

Minor Updates

File Formatting

To accomodate our new developers in-game, we've converted all our 3D models from .blend to .fbx internally.
This should not affect normal players, please let us know if you encounter any broken models or collisions and we'll do our best to resolve it quicky.

A Helpful Hand

We've completely redone the handhold layouts in both A Helpful Hand levels, to accomodate our file format shift.

Bug Fixes

Index Clipping

We've resolved some Index Clipping / Z-Fighting issues on both A Helpful Hand maps, please let us know if you encounter the issues again!


As always, thank you for playing Compensation VR, and have fun! We're looking to host some Q&A sessions in-game in the lounge, so be on the lookout for those if you have any questions!
