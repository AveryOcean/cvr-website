How to Download CVR


CompensationVR is currently available on PCVR and Oculus Quest Platforms. On this page you will find out how to download them.

PCVR Download


In order to download CompensationVR on PCVR head to the games itch.io page and download the file labled "Windows", after downloading the file open it in either 7-Zip or WinRAR


and unzip it into a specific folder for the game. After extracting the CVR Game from the download, if you are on Oculus launch the Oculus App, or if you are on a headset with built-in Steam (e.g.: HTC Vive, Valve Index) support launch SteamVR.


From there on you can launch CompensationVR, make an account and enjoy the game!

Oculus Quest Download

To download CompensationVR on Oculus Quest head to either the games itch.io page. On itch you download the file labled "Android", after doing so you must use a software like SideQuest.

First connect your VR to your Computer / Phone. You go into SideQuest and on the top-right of the app it will show some icons. Click on an icon that looks like a box with a downwards arrow painted on it, and select the downloaded file (cvr.apk).

Then you can go into your Quest -> Games -> Unknown Apps -> CompensationVR. Then you can enjoy the game!
