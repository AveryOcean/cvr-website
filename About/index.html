About Vigor XR

Vigor XR is an online social multiplayer game for PCVR and Oculus headsets.
Our goal is to keep games replayable and empower our players to create anything they like.

Why join Vigor?

  • Community


    Vigor XR has an amazing community so far, with solid moderation and plenty of people willing to help new players.

  • User Generated Content


    Vigor XR has a multitude of creation tools available to players, allowing you to create anything you can imagine.

  • Economy


    Vigor XR has no microtransactions or subscriptions, and is completely free, forever.

  • Open Source Projects


    Almost every single one of our external tools is free and open source, especially those we make ourselves.
    We're not going to smother our competition, because competition and collaboration is necessary for games to improve.

  • Replayability


    We do our best at Vigor to make Original games as replayable as possible, and strive for quality over quantity.

  • Transparency


    We do the absolute most we can at Vigor to be transparent. We're completely willing to explain what's giving us problems, or even ask for help in the community.
    We're willing to accept that we will have bugs, and instead focus on preventing and fixing them.
