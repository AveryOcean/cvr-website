About Vigor XR

Vigor XR is an online social multiplayer game for PCVR and Oculus headsets.
Our goal is to make a fun, enjoyable space for all to create and gather.
We also are working on creation tools to allow players to make whatever they can imagine.
With multiple modes of movement, plenty of room to expand, a great community and plenty to do, why not join?
