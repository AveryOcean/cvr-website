Welcome to Compensation VR



About


Compensation VR is a social VR multiplayer game, designed for friends to hang out and enjoy themselves.
Whether you're a new player, or a returning veteran, welcome!


Getting started


To get started in Compensation VR, simply download the PCVR Launcher, or install the game through Oculus App Lab.
From there, you can launch the game. Press "Register", type in a username, password, nickname, and your age*, and press Register!
After you press register, you will be taken to your Apartment. This is a small space where you can relax and prepare for a nice day in Compensation VR.
Press the "Watch" button on your wrist to open the menu, and you can explore Post Vigor as you see fit!
