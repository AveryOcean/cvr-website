What makes us stand out?

Collaboration

We do our best to share everything we can with both our community and other developers. The only way for games and computers to get better over time is if we collaborate and help each other. That's why we open source every single tool we develop, and aren't afraid to take advice from other developers.

Planning

We put time and effort into the planning of every update and feature we make, before we even touch a key or make a model. We take feedback from our community to the best of our ability and aren't afraid to take advice from other developers.

Testing

We test every single feature thoroughly before we even think about releasing it, making sure you have the best possible experience.

Community

We have an amazing community of playtesters and even other developers who help new players and even contribute to the game on occasion.

Economy

Compensation VR has no microtransactions, and it will stay that way. No subscriptions, no buying currency, nothing.

Feedback

We love to hear suggestions from players, and we don't just write everything off as coming soon or delayed.
