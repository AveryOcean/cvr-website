Creation Tools Demo

What

Compensation VR is hosting a demonstration of it's creation tools, in a live event! We're going to be going over a host of features, from mesh editing, to RBGA materials, to direct OBJ file imports!

When

The Creation Tools Demo is going to take place on August 20th 2022, at 1200 PST. Make sure to be on a few minutes in advance!

Who

We're planning to have a few Developers of CVR as well as some of our staff members and members of the community on-stage, however everybody is welcome to watch!

Where

The event is going to be livestreamed on Twitch and YouTube, recorded, and taking place in a public instance in-game. You will be able to join the event by simply going to a public instance of Raven/Amphitheater!
