Contact


You can contact the developers and the moderation team of Compensation VR through the following means:


Discord


You can contact Subsurface Studios through Discord by joining our Discord Server


Email


You can send an email to compensationvr@gmail.com, however this is not as fast to reply as Discord.


In-Game


You can contact Subsurface Studios staff in Compensation VR by looking for people with "Staff", "Developer", or "Community Support Team" over their head.
If you need moderation assistance, look for individuals with "Moderator" over their head, or use the in-game report feature.
