Room Termination

After a review by our moderation team (in the Suspended state) your room has been terminated.

What does this mean?

We found one or more unnacceptable violations of our Creator Code of Conduct (CCoC) in your room, and we were unable to manually remedy them. Therefore, access to your room has been revoked from all users of Compensation, including you.

Can I appeal this decision?

If you have absolute 100% certainty that your room contained no CCoC violations, or you are willing to remedy all of them yourself, we may choose to reinstate your room, or investigate it a second time.

How can I appeal?

Please contact lead UGC developer raven._.dev on our Discord. You will need to verify your identity and inform them of your Room ID you would like to appeal, and you can expect a decision on your appeal within 10 business days.
